About me

Front-end developer from Antananarivo, Madagascar

My name is Fleuria ARISON, and I proudly hail from the beautiful island nation of Madagascar. At 30 years old, I find joy and purpose in both my personal and professional life. I am blessed to be married and have the privilege of being a mother to three wonderful children.

My journey into the world of the web began in 2018, and since then, it has become an integral part of my life. Recently, I was fortunate enough to partake in a free coding course at SheCodes.

The training was not only rich in content but was also an incredibly fun experience. It was there that I discovered a burgeoning passion for coding, particularly in the area of front-end development.

Now, as I stand on the brink of a new chapter, I am filled with anticipation. The digital realm beckons, and I'm eager to establish myself as a front-end developer. The future is bright, and I can't wait to see where this new passion leads me.
